### (b)
$x_{2}(t) = e^{j(2t + \frac{\pi}{4})}$

Energy:
$$
\begin{align*}
E &= \int^{t_{2}}_{t_{1}} |x(t)|^{2} \, dt \\
&= \int^{t_{2}}_{t_{1}} |e^{j\left( 2t + \frac{\pi}{4} \right)}|^{2} \, dt \\
&= \int^{t_{2}}_{t_{1}} 1 \, dt \\
&= [t]_{t_{1}}^{t_{2}}\\
&= t_{2}-t_{1}
\end{align*}
$$

Power:
$$
\begin{align*}
P &= \frac{1}{t_{2} - t_{1}} \int^{t_{2}}_{t_{1}} |x(t)|^{2} \, dt \\
&= \frac{1}{t_{2} - t_{1}} \cdot t_{2}-t_{1}\\
&= 1
\end{align*}
$$
